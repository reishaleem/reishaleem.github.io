Advanced Energy Vehicle (AEV)

Language(s):
Arduino

The AEV project was assigned by the first year engineering class at Ohio State. My team consisted of 4 members. The design for the AEV was made using SolidWorks. Students were given the option to 3D print parts of the AEV that were not available in the given lab kit. The goal of the project was to have the AEV traverse a track and complete the following steps: stop before a gate and activate a motion sensor, wait for the gate to lift, continue through the track, pick up a piece of cargo by using a magnet, and then come back to the beginning, once again going through the gate. The AEV was programmed with an Arduino.
